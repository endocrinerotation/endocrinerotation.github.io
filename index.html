Curriculum:

There are several modules that you will study during your rotation, and there will be a series of QUIZ tests on these modules.

To access the modules: ENDOCRINE_MODULES

You will take the quiz test on "diabetes module" on day # 1 of rotation. I would, therefore, recommend that you start studying "diabetes module" BEFORE you start your rotation.
